What is Pet Care?

Pet Care is an online platform that connects
you with trusted groomers and veterinarians
in Jakarta. We are committed to providing the
best pet care services at affordable prices

Who made Pet Care?

The team behind Pet Care is a group of passionate
university students. Combining their love for
animals with their academic backgrounds in tech,
design, and business, they've built this platform to
simplify pet care for Jakarta residents.
